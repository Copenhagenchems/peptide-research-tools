## Reconstitution Quick Math

**Formula:** `Concentration (mcg/ml) = Vial content (mcg) ÷ BAC water added (ml)`

| Vial | BAC Water | Concentration | Units for 250mcg dose |
|------|-----------|---------------|-----------------------|
| 5mg | 2ml | 2,500 mcg/ml | 10 units |
| 5mg | 1ml | 5,000 mcg/ml | 5 units |
| 10mg | 2ml | 5,000 mcg/ml | 5 units |
| 2mg | 1ml | 2,000 mcg/ml | 12.5 units |

## Storage

| State | Temperature | Shelf Life |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| Lyophilized (sealed) | -20°C | 24+ months |
| Lyophilized (room temp) | <25°C | 3-6 months |
| Reconstituted (BAC water) | 2-8°C | 28-30 days |
| Reconstituted (sterile water) | 2-8°C | 24-48 hours |

Never freeze reconstituted peptides.
